About Me

I'm a senior at Papillion-LaVista High School and I live a very hectic lifestyle. Throughout my high school career I’ve taken on several leadership roles. Starting with sophomore year, I embraced the challenge of being the lead light and sound technician in my school’s auditorium. Progressing into junior year, I put more pressure on myself by becoming an exec liaison for the International Thespian Society and President of our Skills USA chapter. Lastly, in my senior year I have taken on the responsibility to lead the Broadcasting Club, lead the Robotics Club, intern at a highly selective program for Gallup, and be the sole up keeper of the school’s journalism website.
